# Terms and Conditions

<!-- tags: terms, conditions, legal, T&C, booking conditions -->

Your terms and conditions are displayed during the booking process and must be accepted by the customer before confirming.

## Edit

1. Go to **Supporting Texts → Terms and Conditions**.
2. Edit the text (HTML formatting supported).
3. Click **Save**.
4. Flush HTML cache to publish changes immediately.

## Versioning

GOL IBE stores previous versions of your T&C.  
To view history: click **Show history** in the top right corner.

> ⚠️ Consult your legal team before making changes to terms and conditions.
