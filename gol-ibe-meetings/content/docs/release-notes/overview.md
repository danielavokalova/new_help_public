# Release notes

What's new in GOL IBE Meetings.

## May 2026

- **Help portal launched** — full-screen portal with sidebar navigation, search, and walkthroughs.
- **Contact Help form** — submit support requests directly from the portal.
- **App switcher** — quick links to Meetings Console, TCP, and My Travelport.

## April 2026

- **Initial release** — GOL IBE Meetings is now available.
- **Meeting requests** — create, edit, and cancel meeting requests with full workflow support.
- **Attendee management** — invite internal and external attendees, collect travel preferences.
- **Approval workflows** — configurable single- and multi-level approvals with escalation rules.
- **Integrated travel booking** — search and book flights, hotels, and transfers from within the meeting.
- **Reports** — meeting summary, travel spend, cost centre breakdown, and CSV export.
