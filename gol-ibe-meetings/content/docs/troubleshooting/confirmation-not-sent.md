# Booking confirmation not sent

An attendee says they did not receive the booking confirmation email.

## Checklist

1. **Check the delivery log** at **Settings → Notifications → Delivery log**.
2. If the email **Bounced**, the attendee's email address is incorrect. Update it under **Attendees → [Attendee name] → Edit**.
3. If the email shows **Delivered**, ask the attendee to check their spam folder.
4. **Resend** the confirmation from the delivery log if needed.
