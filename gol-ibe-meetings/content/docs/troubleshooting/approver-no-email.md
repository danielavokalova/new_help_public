# Approver not receiving notifications

The approver says they have not received the approval request email.

## Checklist

1. **Check the delivery log** — go to **Settings → Notifications → Delivery log** and look for the email. If the status is **Bounced**, the email address is wrong.
2. **Verify the approver's email address** — go to **Settings → Users**, open the approver's profile, and confirm the email is correct.
3. **Check spam/junk folder** — ask the approver to check their spam folder and whitelist the sender domain.
4. **Confirm the approver is assigned to the cost centre** — go to **Settings → Approvers** and verify the mapping.
5. **Resend the notification** — in the delivery log, find the failed email and click **Resend**.
