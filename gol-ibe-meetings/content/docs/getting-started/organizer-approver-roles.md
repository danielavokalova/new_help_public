# Organiser vs. approver roles

Understanding the difference between these two roles helps you set up users correctly.

## Organiser

An organiser is responsible for planning the meeting:
- Creates and edits meeting requests.
- Adds attendees and collects travel preferences.
- Submits the request for approval.
- Monitors the status through to completion.

## Approver

An approver reviews and authorises spending:
- Receives notifications when a meeting request is submitted.
- Can approve, reject, or request more information.
- May be assigned to one or more cost centres.
- Can delegate their approval authority temporarily (e.g. during holiday).

## Can someone have both roles?

Yes. An **Admin** user has full access to both organiser and approver functions. A regular user can only hold one role at a time — assign the role that matches their primary responsibility.
