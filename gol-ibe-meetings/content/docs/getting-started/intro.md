# What is GOL IBE Meetings?

**GOL IBE Meetings** is a corporate travel management module built on the GOL IBE platform. It lets travel managers and event organisers create meeting requests, invite attendees, and automatically book the travel required to bring everyone together.

## Key capabilities

- **Meeting requests** — Create a structured request with dates, location, purpose, and budget.
- **Attendee management** — Invite internal and external participants; collect travel preferences automatically.
- **Approval workflows** — Route requests to approvers, set thresholds, and track approval status in real time.
- **Integrated bookings** — Search and book flights, hotels, and transfers directly from within the meeting.
- **Notifications** — Automated email notifications at every stage: invitation, approval, booking confirmation, and cancellation.
- **Reporting** — Track travel spend by meeting, cost centre, or department.

## Who uses GOL IBE Meetings?

| Role | Typical tasks |
|---|---|
| **Meeting organiser** | Creates requests, invites attendees, monitors status |
| **Approver** | Reviews and approves or rejects meeting requests |
| **Travel agent** | Books flights, hotels, and transfers for confirmed meetings |
| **Administrator** | Configures the system, manages users and cost centres |
