# Invite external attendees

External attendees are participants who do not have a GOL IBE Meetings account — typically clients, partners, or guest speakers.

## Steps

1. In the **Attendees** tab of a meeting, click **Add attendee**.
2. Enter the external person's **first name**, **last name**, and **email address**.
3. Check **External attendee**.
4. Click **Save**.

## What the external attendee receives

- An email invitation with meeting details.
- A secure link to a self-service page where they can confirm attendance and enter travel preferences.
- They do **not** get a login to the GOL IBE Meetings admin console.
