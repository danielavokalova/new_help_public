# Approve or reject a meeting request

As an approver you can action requests directly from the email notification or from the console.

## From the email notification

1. Open the approval email and click **Review request**.
2. Check the meeting details — dates, location, attendees, budget.
3. Click **Approve** or **Reject**.
4. Optionally add a comment for the organiser.
5. Click **Confirm**.

## From the console

1. Go to **Approvals → Pending** in the navigation.
2. Click the meeting request you want to review.
3. Click **Approve** or **Reject** in the top-right corner.
4. Add a comment if needed and confirm.

## After your decision

| Action | Organiser notified? | Next step |
|---|---|---|
| Approved | Yes | Travel agent books flights and hotels |
| Rejected | Yes | Organiser can revise and re-submit |
