# Create a meeting request

A meeting request captures all the information needed to plan travel for a corporate event.

## Prerequisites

- You have organiser access (role: **Organiser** or **Admin**).
- At least one cost centre is configured in [Configuration → Cost centres](/portal/configuration/cost-centres).

## Steps

1. Go to **Meetings** in the left navigation and click **New meeting**.
2. Fill in the meeting details:
   - **Title** — descriptive name for the meeting.
   - **Start date / End date** — full date and time, including time zone.
   - **Location** — city, venue name, or address.
   - **Purpose** — select from the dropdown (e.g. Client visit, Internal training, Conference).
   - **Cost centre** — assigns the meeting to a budget.
3. Add a description and any special requirements in the **Notes** field.
4. Click **Save draft** to save without submitting, or **Submit for approval** to send to the assigned approver.

## After submission

- The meeting status changes to **Pending approval**.
- The approver receives an email notification.
- You can track progress under **Meetings → My requests**.
