# Remove an attendee

You can remove an attendee from a meeting at any point before their travel has been issued.

## Steps

1. Open the meeting and go to the **Attendees** tab.
2. Find the attendee you want to remove.
3. Click the **...** menu next to their name and select **Remove**.
4. Confirm in the dialog.

The attendee receives a notification that they have been removed from the meeting.

> If a ticket has already been issued for this attendee, you must cancel the booking separately before removing them.
