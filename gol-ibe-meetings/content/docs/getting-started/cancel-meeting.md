# Cancel a meeting request

You can cancel a meeting request at any time before travel has been issued.

## Steps

1. Open **Meetings → My requests**.
2. Click the meeting you want to cancel.
3. Click **Cancel meeting** and confirm in the dialog.

## What happens next

- The meeting status changes to **Cancelled**.
- Attendees receive a cancellation notification by email.
- Any pending bookings linked to the meeting are flagged for manual review.

> **Note:** If tickets have already been issued, contact your travel agent to arrange refunds or re-use of the ticket.
