# Attendee travel preferences

Travel preferences are stored on the attendee profile and applied automatically when booking travel for a meeting.

## Preferences you can store

- **Seat preference** — aisle, window, or no preference
- **Meal preference** — standard, vegetarian, vegan, kosher, halal, etc.
- **Frequent flyer numbers** — per airline
- **Hotel loyalty numbers** — per hotel chain
- **Passport details** — required for international travel

## How to update preferences

1. Go to **Attendees** and open the attendee's profile.
2. Click **Edit preferences**.
3. Update the relevant fields and click **Save**.

> Attendees can also update their own preferences by logging in and going to **My profile → Travel preferences**.
