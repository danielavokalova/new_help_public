# Add attendees to a meeting

After creating a meeting request you can add the people who need to attend.

## Steps

1. Open the meeting from **Meetings → My requests**.
2. Click the **Attendees** tab.
3. Click **Add attendee** and search by name or email address.
4. Select the attendee from the results.
5. Optionally set their **role**: Presenter, Participant, or Optional.
6. Click **Save**.

## Bulk import

To add many attendees at once:
1. Click **Import from CSV**.
2. Download the template, fill in the required columns (first name, last name, email), and upload it.
3. Review the preview and click **Confirm**.

> Attendees are notified by email once the meeting is **approved**.
