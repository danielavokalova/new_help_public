# Cancellation notification

Sent to attendees when a meeting or their travel booking is cancelled.

## When it is triggered

- The organiser cancels the meeting.
- A travel agent cancels a specific booking for this attendee.
- The approver rejects the meeting request.

## Default content

- Meeting title and dates
- Reason for cancellation (if provided)
- Contact details for the organiser
