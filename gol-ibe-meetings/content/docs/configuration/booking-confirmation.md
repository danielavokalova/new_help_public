# Booking confirmation email

Sent to an attendee after their travel (flight, hotel, or transfer) has been booked.

## Default content

- Passenger name and travel details (flight number, dates, PNR)
- Hotel name, check-in/check-out dates (if applicable)
- Meeting name and location
