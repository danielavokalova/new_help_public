# Service fees

Service fees are charges added on top of the fare to cover your agency's handling costs.

## Create a service fee

1. Go to **Settings → Service fees** and click **Add fee**.
2. Set the **name** (e.g. "Meetings handling fee").
3. Choose the **type**:
   - **Fixed** — a flat amount per booking.
   - **Percentage** — a percentage of the total fare.
4. Set the **value** and **currency**.
5. Optionally restrict to a specific **meeting type** or **cost centre**.
6. Click **Save**.

## Applying fees

Service fees are applied automatically during the booking step. They appear as a separate line item on the invoice.
