# Agency profile setup

The agency profile stores your organisation's details and is used in email templates and invoices.

## Steps

1. Go to **Settings → Agency profile**.
2. Fill in the required fields:
   - **Company name**
   - **Address**
   - **VAT / registration number**
   - **Support email** — displayed in notification footers
   - **Phone number**
3. Upload your **logo** (recommended: PNG, 400 × 120 px, transparent background).
4. Click **Save**.
