# Delegate approval authority

When you are unavailable, you can temporarily delegate your approval authority to another user.

## Steps

1. Go to **Settings → My approvals** or ask an Admin to open **Settings → Approvers**.
2. Click **Delegate authority**.
3. Select the **delegate** (must be a user with the Approver or Admin role).
4. Set the **delegation period** — start date and end date.
5. Click **Save**.

During the delegation period, all new requests routed to you are also sent to your delegate. Both of you can approve or reject.

## Ending a delegation early

1. Open **Settings → Approvers**.
2. Find the active delegation and click **End delegation**.
