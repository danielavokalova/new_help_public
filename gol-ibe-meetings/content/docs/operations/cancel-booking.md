# Cancel a travel booking

Cancel a flight, hotel, or transfer for one or more attendees.

## Steps

1. Open the meeting and go to the **Travel** tab.
2. Find the booking and click **Cancel**.
3. Select whether to cancel for **this attendee only** or **all attendees**.
4. Confirm the cancellation.

The attendee receives a cancellation email. The cancellation fee (if any) is shown before you confirm.

> Cancelling travel does not cancel the meeting itself. Use [Cancel a meeting request](/portal/getting-started/cancel-meeting) if you want to cancel the entire meeting.
