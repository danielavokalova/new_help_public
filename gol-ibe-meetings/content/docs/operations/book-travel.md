# Book travel for a meeting

Once a meeting is approved, travel agents can search and book flights and hotels for attendees.

## Prerequisites

- The meeting status is **Approved**.
- You have the **Travel agent** or **Admin** role.
- A [GDS connector](/portal/configuration/gds-connector) is configured.

## Steps

1. Open the meeting from **Meetings → All meetings**.
2. Click the **Travel** tab.
3. Click **Book travel** next to the attendee you want to book for.
4. Select the travel type: **Flight**, **Hotel**, or **Transfer**.
5. Use the search form to find available options.
6. Select the preferred result and click **Book**.
7. Review the booking summary and confirm.

The attendee receives a booking confirmation email and the meeting status updates to **Booked** (if not already).
