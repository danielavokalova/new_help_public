# Airport transfers

Arrange ground transfers for attendees arriving by air.

## Steps

1. Open the meeting, go to the **Travel** tab, and click **Book travel**.
2. Select the attendee and choose **Transfer**.
3. Enter the pick-up point (airport or hotel), destination, and date/time.
4. Select the vehicle type (sedan, minivan, etc.).
5. Confirm the booking.
