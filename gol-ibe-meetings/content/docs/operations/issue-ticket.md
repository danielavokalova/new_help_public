# Issue a ticket for a meeting

Issue the electronic ticket (e-ticket) for a booked flight.

## Prerequisites

- The flight is in **Confirmed** booking status.
- You have the **Travel agent** or **Admin** role.
- The payment method is set up and the payment due date has not passed.

## Steps

1. Open the meeting and go to the **Travel** tab.
2. Find the flight booking and click **Issue ticket**.
3. Confirm the passenger details and payment method.
4. Click **Issue**.

The e-ticket number appears in the booking details and the attendee receives a confirmation email with the ticket attached.
