# Hotel bookings

Search and book hotels for meeting attendees.

## Steps

1. Open the meeting, go to the **Travel** tab, and click **Book travel**.
2. Select the attendee and choose **Hotel**.
3. Enter the destination, check-in date, check-out date, and number of rooms.
4. Browse results and click **Book** on the preferred option.
5. Confirm the details.

## Tips

- Attendee hotel loyalty numbers from their profile are applied automatically during checkout.
- Use the **Filter** panel to narrow results by star rating, price, or distance from the meeting venue.
