# How to create footer on your frontend?

1\) Log into admin console.

2\) Go to the section **Supporting texts - Supporting texts**.

3\) Click at button **"Add text"**.

4\) Start with footer creation.

a) **Name** - we recommed to call this type Footer. This way you will recognize it easily in the list of supporting texts.

b) **Text type** - find Footer.

c) **Language** - choose that one your frontend is in.

d) **Body of footer** - insert whatever text you would like to have in your footer.
