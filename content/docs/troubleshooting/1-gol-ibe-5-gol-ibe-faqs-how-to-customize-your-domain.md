# How to customize your domain?

In case you are having the highest version of GOL IBE - GOL IBE enterprise you are welcome to customize your domain. It means that instead of  **something**.golibe.com your GOL IBE frontend will be under your own link. 

**How to proceed with that?** 

1\) You tell us what your custom domain is.

2\) You adjust your domain record and add there: your domain CNAME [proxy.golibe.com](http://proxy.golibe.com) .

3\) You let us know that this is done on your side.

4\) We will take care of the rest.
