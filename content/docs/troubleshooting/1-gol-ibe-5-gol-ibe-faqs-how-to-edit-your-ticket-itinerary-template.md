# How to edit your ticket itinerary template?

To edit your ticket itinerary template: 

1. Log into administration console.
2. To edit template click button **EDIT.**



![](/images/docs/8d5af359bd91bfb2.png)



3\. Editaton

3a) Simple editation

 To make simple editations just click at blue window you find on a task bar menu.

 Ticket itinerary will be fully at your disposal.

 Just click into the body of template and start with editation. ( You may add/change/delete text or logo. )

 Once done just save changes.



![](/images/docs/084ffd7cc139e97f.png)



If done, click on blue window on task bar menu agai**n** and save changes.

3b) Editation via HTML 

If you prefer editation via HTML just click on HTML on template task bar menu and go ahead.



![](/images/docs/401f2d6e6fba80f7.png)





![](/images/docs/00f7cd50851db3a4.png)
