# How to export the list of bookings?

* Log into the admin console.
* Go to the section **Reservations**.
* Specify time period you would like to get report for in section **Created since: / Created till:**
* Choose one of the option for exporting.



![](/images/docs/fb590e7e29f4283c.png)





![](/images/docs/853e86168e7e28b9.png)



* We strongly recommend to cancel all bookings their due date is over and remained unpaid. 
* We recommend to check out the list of bookings systematically on every day basis.
* If you keep bookings active and will not be canceled by you it may happen that carriers will charge you by ADM. Also they may block your account.
