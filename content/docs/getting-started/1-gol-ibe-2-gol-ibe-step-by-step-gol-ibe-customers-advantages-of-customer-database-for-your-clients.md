# Advantages of customer database for your clients

<!-- tags: customer database, benefits, passengers, registration, repeat booking, travel documents -->

When customers register on your GOL IBE booking engine, they gain several benefits that make the booking experience faster and more convenient.

## Key benefits for customers

### 1. Pre-filled booking forms

Registered customers do not have to re-enter their name, date of birth, contact details, or travel document numbers every time they book. GOL IBE pre-fills the passenger form automatically from their saved profile.

### 2. Stored travel documents

Customers can save multiple travel documents (passports, ID cards) to their profile. When booking a flight that requires specific document types, the correct document is already available to select.

### 3. Booking history

Customers can log in and view all their past and upcoming bookings in one place — without searching through their email inbox for confirmations.

### 4. Faster checkout

Because personal data is already on file, customers move through the booking flow more quickly, reducing the chance of abandoning the booking mid-way.

### 5. Password-protected account

Customer data is protected behind a personal account — other users cannot see or modify their bookings or personal details.

## How customers access their account

Customers click **Log in** on your booking engine front-end and use the email and password they set during registration.

## Tips

- Encourage customers to register by highlighting the time-saving benefit at checkout
- Customers can update their own profiles after logging in — you do not need to do it for them
- If a customer forgets their password, they can reset it via the **Forgot password** link on the login page
