# Where can you insert your agency URL under your logo?

<!-- tags: agency URL, logo link, front-end, branding, homepage link, dealer -->

You can set a clickable link on your agency logo so that when customers click the logo on your GOL IBE booking engine, they are taken to your main agency website.

## How to set the logo URL (clickable link)

1. Log into the GOL IBE admin console.
2. Go to **Dealers → Dealers** → your dealer → **Front-end settings**.
3. Find the field **Logo link URL** (or **Agency URL / Homepage URL**).
4. Enter your agency website URL (e.g. `https://www.youragency.com`).
5. Click **Save**.
6. Go to **Code Lists → Flush HTML Caches**.

## Result

The logo in the header of your booking engine becomes a clickable link that opens your agency homepage. This is standard on all professional booking engines and helps customers navigate between your main site and the booking engine.

## Tips

- Use `https://` — avoid plain `http://` for security and browser trust
- If you want the link to open in a **new tab**, contact CEE Systems support — this requires a configuration change
- Make sure the destination URL is always reachable; a broken logo link looks unprofessional
- This field is separate from the **Logo URL** field (which is the image source) — both need to be filled in
