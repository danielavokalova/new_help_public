# Markup Rules and Service Fees

<!-- tags: markup, service fee, prices, commission, discount, agency -->

GOL IBE gives you several tools in the **Prices** section to control how your agency earns on bookings.

## Service fees

Service fees are added to the ticket price shown to customers. You can set fixed or percentage-based fees with conditions such as carrier, passenger type, cabin class, or route.

Navigate to **Prices → Service fees – agency** (or **Service fees – dealers** for dealer-specific fees).

See the full setup guide: [How to set up service fee](/portal/configuration/1-gol-ibe-3-gol-ibe-basic-settings-how-to-set-up-service-fee)

## Commissions and discounts

Airlines may give commissions on sold tickets. You can configure these under **Prices → Commissions and discounts**.

Rules can be set per carrier, route, cabin class, flight restrictions, and time period. Rules are applied in priority order.

See: [Commissions and discounts](/portal/getting-started/1-gol-ibe-2-gol-ibe-step-by-step-prices-what-is-section-commissions-and-discounts-good-for)

## Promo codes

Promotional codes are part of higher GOL IBE editions. Configure them under **Prices → Commissions and discounts** using the discount type **Discount from total price & Promo code**.

See: [How to manage promo codes](/portal/getting-started/1-gol-ibe-2-gol-ibe-step-by-step-prices-how-to-manage-promocodes)
