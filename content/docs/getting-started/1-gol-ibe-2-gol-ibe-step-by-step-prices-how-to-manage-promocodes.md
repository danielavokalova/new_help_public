# How to manage promocodes?

***

Promocodes feature is part of the higher version of GOL IBE. 

* GOL IBE enhanced
* GOL IBE enterprise

How to create a rule for a promo in admin console? 

* Log into admin console.
* Go to the section **Price - Commissions and discounts.**
* Press button **"Add a rule".**
* Agency name has to be preset
* You may specify priority
* Choose the type: **Discount from total price & Promo code**
* Continue with creating the whole rule by choosing type of the discount (fixed or in %)
* Specify the type and height of your discount by section **Commission / Discount amount**
