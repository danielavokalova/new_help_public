# Reservations

<!-- tags: reservations, bookings, cancel, export, handle, clients -->

The **Reservations** section is where all flight bookings are managed. Bookings are created by your customers through the GOL IBE front-end and appear here automatically.

## What you can do here

- **View** booking details — open the full booking including the ticket itinerary (DETAIL)
- **Edit** a booking — change payment status or add your own remarks (EDIT)
- **Cancel** bookings — cancellation is applied immediately and also in Smartpoint (CANCEL)
- **Export** booking lists to CSV or Excel for reporting
- **Handle bookings with clients** — verify identity, discuss payment, and forward the full confirmation
- **Block fake bookings** by adding email addresses to the blocked list

Use the articles below to learn how to handle each type of reservation action.
