# Statistics

***

You are welcome to download statistics about bookings made via your GOL IBE front-end.

1\) Log into admin console.

2\) Find section called **Statistics**.

3\) Choose one of the options system offers you (in CSV or in Excel).

4\) Statistics are downloaded in a moment.



![](/images/docs/b353c6389d0a0821.png)



***

Description of all columns that appear in our statistics:

* **Name** - what it represents 
* **Id** - serial number of the record
* **Connector** - the source of booking data 
* **Status** - current booking status
* **Traveller** - quantity of passengers excluding infants
* **Infant** - quantity of infants 
* **Agency** - agency name
* **Agency country** - agency country of origin
* **Dealer** - dealer name
* **Reservation date** - date of booking
* **Reservation year** - the year when booking created
* **Reservation month** - the month when booking created 
* **From** - departure point
* **City from** - departure city 
* **Country from** -departure country 
* **Where** - airport IATA code
* **City where** - destination / parent IATA code of the airport
* **Country where** - destination country code of airport location
* **Type** - journey type
* **Departure** - departure date
* **Arrival** - arrival date
* **Reservation segment** - quantity of flights in the itinerary multiplied by the number of passengers (excluding infants)
* **Itinerary segment** - number of flights in the itinerary
* **Base price** - the fare price
* **Total price** - total price
* **Service fee price** - service fee amount
