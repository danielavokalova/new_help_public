# How to manage terms and conditions?

To update your agency Terms and conditions that appear on your front-end:

1\) Log into administration console.

2\) Go to the section **Supporting texts - Supporting texts**.

3\) Find template for Terms and conditions (Text type called - **Ticket search - Travel conditions**).

4\) If you prefer plain text just insert text into the white space and save changes.
