# Blocked E-mails

<!-- tags: blocked email, spam, blacklist, email, bounce, block -->

The Blocked E-mails list prevents booking confirmations and notifications from being sent to specific addresses.

## When to use

- Email address is invalid or bouncing
- Customer has requested to be unsubscribed
- Address is flagged as spam trap

## How to block an email

1. Go to **Code Lists → Blocked E-mails → Add address**.
2. Enter the email address.
3. Optionally add a note (reason for blocking).
4. Click **Save**.

## Remove a blocked email

1. Find the address in the list.
2. Click the **trash icon** to remove it.

> ℹ️ Removing an address from the block list restores normal email delivery immediately.
