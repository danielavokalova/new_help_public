# Company billing details (optional) and its management

<!-- tags: billing details, company, invoice, VAT, dealer, optional -->

In addition to the agency-level billing details, each **dealer** can optionally have its own company billing information. This is useful when you manage multiple brands or sub-agencies under one GOL IBE account, each invoicing customers under a different legal entity.

## When to use dealer-level billing details

- You operate **multiple branded front-ends** (dealers) under one GOL IBE account
- Each brand has a different **legal name** or **VAT number**
- Customers of dealer A should receive invoices from Company A, not from your main agency

## How to configure billing details for a dealer

1. Log into the GOL IBE admin console.
2. Go to **Dealers → Dealers** and open the dealer record.
3. Click **Edit** (Dealer Edit).
4. Scroll to the **Billing details** section (may be labelled **Company details** or **Invoice details**).
5. Fill in the fields:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Company name** | Legal name for this dealer's invoices |
| **Address** | Street address |
| **City / ZIP / Country** | Location details |
| **VAT number** | VAT registration for this company |
| **Invoice prefix** | Prefix for invoice numbers (e.g. `BRAND2-`) |

6. Click **Save**.

## Tips

- If left empty, invoices use the **agency-level** billing details from **Agency → Agency detail**
- Dealer-level billing details override agency details only for bookings made through that dealer's front-end
- Changes take effect on the next invoice generated — already issued invoices are not affected
