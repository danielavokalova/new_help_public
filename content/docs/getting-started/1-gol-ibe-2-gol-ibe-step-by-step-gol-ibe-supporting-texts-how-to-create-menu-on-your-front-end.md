# How to create menu on your front-end?

To prepare your menu:

1. Log into admin console.
2. Go to the section **Supporting texts - Supporting texts**.
3. Press button **"Add text"**.
4. Start with creating the first part of your menu.

- Insert the name of your menu.
- Choose your Dealer.
- Choose **Text type** (we recommend starting from Menu 1 and continuing with Menu 2, etc.).
- Specify the language your front-end is in.
- In case you would like to put a URL link under that part of the menu, just insert a URL in the section **Alternative URL.**
- In case you would like to just insert plain text under that menu, insert whatever text you wish in the white part under the grey task bar menu.
- Save changes.

This way you have prepared the 1st part of your menu.

If you would like to continue with adding more parts, just continue the same way.

Once all parts are prepared (see an example below), go to the section **Supporting text - Menu setting.**

Being there, select your agency name in the section Dealer.

Choose all the menu parts you just prepared in the left box. One by one, click on each. Then click the arrow pointing left. This will move each part of your menu from the left box to the right box. Once done (as per the example in the picture), press **Save menu**.

This way your menu will be published.

Anytime you would like to remove your menu from your front-end, go to the section **Supporting text - Menu setting** and move the published parts from the right box back to the left one. As soon as you save changes the menu disappears from your front-end.
