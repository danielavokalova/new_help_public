# How to change background on your front-end?

<!-- tags: background, image, colour, front-end, design, dealer, customisation -->

You can change the background of your GOL IBE booking engine to a custom image or colour that matches your brand.

## Steps

1. Log into the GOL IBE admin console.
2. Go to **Dealers** and select the dealer whose front-end you want to update.
3. Click **Edit → Front-end settings**.
4. Find the **Background** section.
5. Choose your background type:
   - **Solid colour** — enter a hex colour code (e.g. `#f5f7fb`)
   - **Image** — upload a background image (JPG or PNG recommended)
6. If uploading an image:
   - Recommended size: **1920×1080 px** or larger
   - File size: keep under **500 KB** for fast loading
   - Position: choose **Cover** (fills the screen) or **Tile** (repeats)
7. Click **Save**.
8. Go to **Code Lists → Flush HTML Caches** to apply the change immediately.

## Tips

- Use a slightly dark or blurred image so the search form remains readable
- Test on both desktop and mobile — background images may crop differently
- A subtle pattern or gradient often works better than a busy photograph
