# Terms and Conditions

<!-- tags: terms, conditions, legal, T&C, booking conditions, supporting texts -->

Your terms and conditions are displayed during the booking process and must be accepted by the customer before confirming.

## Edit

1. Log into the admin console.
2. Go to **Supporting texts → Supporting texts**.
3. Find the template with text type **Ticket search – Travel conditions**.
4. To use plain text: type or paste your text into the white area and save.
5. To link to an external page: insert the URL in the **Alternative URL** field.

> Flush HTML caches after saving to publish changes immediately on the front-end.
