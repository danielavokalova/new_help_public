# How to manage calendar on your GOL IBE web?

This feature enables you to offer flight tickets from certain time on base the date when booking is made. 

Have a look below.

* Go to admin console
* Find section Dealers - EDIT
* Scroll down to the section FLIGHT SEARCH and find section ENABLE SEARCH FROM.
* Choose an option
* Save changes.

<div align="left">

![](/images/docs/efd6acd770542b75.png)

</div>

What does that mean? 

Actual day - you offer the flights even for the day when booking is made

A day before - you offer flights from tommorow.

2 days before - you offer flights in 2 days from today.

3 days before - you offer flights in 3 days from today.

And so on.
